Precision Condition Monitoring Platform for MeasureWare Development Kits

MeasureWare’s precision condition monitoring platform, ADMW1001, enables connection to a host of analog and digital sensors like temperature, pressure, humidity, load & strain. It will automatically take care of all the measurement configuration and management. Extensive flexibility means any channel can be configured or reconfigured for any sensor at any time.
